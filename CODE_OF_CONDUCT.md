# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, specific, and constructive.

We want ClawCrew to be a place where contributors can discuss runtime design, safety, OSS usability, and product direction without hostility or personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment or discrimination
- personal attacks
- sustained bad-faith review behavior
- publishing private information without consent

## Maintainer expectations

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate these standards and may restrict participation if needed to protect the project.
